Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Privexa: EXIF & GPS Remover ("the App"), provided by Hiroki Sugiyama ("we", "us"), handles your information. The App is an iOS application that removes location and other metadata from your photos.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to us. There is no account system and no backend server operated by us. All processing happens on your device.

Photos and Photo Library

When you select photos to clean, the App reads them from your device's photo library and processes them only on your device. The cleaned copies are saved back to your photo library on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any server and are not shared with us or any third party.

Location Data

The App reads GPS location data embedded in your photos solely to remove it at your request. The App does not access your device's live location or transmit any location data.

Purchases

Purchases (the one-time lifetime unlock) are processed by Apple through the App Store. To verify and manage your purchase status, the App uses a purchase-management service (RevenueCat), which processes an anonymous identifier and basic purchase/device information. We do not receive your name, email address, or payment card details.

Analytics and Advertising

This version of the App does not include third-party analytics or advertising.

Data Retention and Deletion

All processing is performed on your device. The App does not retain your photos or metadata. Deleting the App from your device removes all App data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hiroki.sugiyama.dev@gmail.com.
